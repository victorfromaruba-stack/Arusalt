A Legacy Revived.

Aruba's rich salt mining history dates back to 1636. For centuries, our island's "white gold" was a cornerstone of the Caribbean.

In 2021, AruSalt was born out of a passion to revive this heritage. We are a family-run company dedicated to traditional, sustainable harvesting methods, bringing the authentic flavor of our "One Happy Island" directly to your table.
