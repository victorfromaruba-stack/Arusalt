The Artisan's
Culinary Guide

Our salt is unrefined, crunchy sea salt, crafted entirely by hand in small batches from the Caribbean ocean. By carefully balancing solar evaporation and exposure to crisp, clean ocean breezes, seawater slowly forms into brilliant white salt crystals with a mild, briny flavor.

Coarse Crystals

Perfectly balanced texture—not too hard and not too soft. Ideal for grinding, seasoning, and elevating the natural flavors of hearty dishes without the metallic notes found in table salt.

Finishing Flakes

Use strictly for finishing to preserve the delicate, wonderfully pleasing crunch. Enhances the natural profile of fresh seafood, salads, or artisan chocolates.

Harmony With Nature

Our traditional process, utilizing open-air salt pans, is entirely natural requiring only the sea, wind, sun, and patience. We evaporate fresh, clean, filtered seawater outdoors. Unlike most other saltmakers, we do not boil or oven-dry seawater, nor trap the forming crystals in a factory building or greenhouse. We use no anti-caking agents, bleaching additives, or other chemical additions.

Aruba's Salt Heritage.

Long before modern roads and resorts transformed the island, Aruba's natural salinas played an important role in everyday life.

Scattered along the coastline and across the island's low-lying landscapes, these shallow salt ponds provided local communities with one of nature's most valuable resources.

Today, AruSalt proudly revives this island tradition.
